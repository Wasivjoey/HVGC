Everything your serving teams need

One place to plan the rota, track training, and keep volunteers in the loop.

🗓️

Smart scheduling

Build a roster for any service. Turn on the optional auto-scheduler to fill roles automatically with people who are trained, available, and not already booked — with the load shared fairly.

🎓

Training & qualifications

Tie required training to each role, so only qualified volunteers get scheduled. Everyone can see what they need to complete to serve.

📅

Availability

Volunteers mark the days they can and can't serve. Schedulers see it at a glance and never double-book someone who's away.

📆

Team calendar

A month-at-a-glance view of every service and everyone serving on it — colour-coded by person or by ministry team.

🔁

Role swaps

Can't make it? Request cover in a tap. Another qualified volunteer picks it up and a team lead approves the swap.

🔔

Reminders & invites

Assignments arrive by email with a calendar invite (.ics) and automatic reminders the day before and an hour before the service.

👥

Teams & leads

Group volunteers into ministry teams. Team leads manage their own people — assigning roles, tracking training, and resetting passwords.

📣

Announcements & polls

Share news with the whole team and gather quick decisions with polls, right on the volunteer dashboard.

How it works

1

Create your account

Sign up and verify your email. A team lead or admin approves you and adds you to a team.

2

Set your roles & availability

Get your serving roles, finish any required training, and mark when you're free.

3

Show up ready

Receive your schedule with calendar invites and reminders, and swap a slot if plans change.
